About 关于

A realm for otome stories, made to be entered. 为乙女故事而生，

Craft 创作

Art, writing, and playable immersion. 美术、文案与可游玩的代入感。

Art美术

I want the visuals to do more than look beautiful. They should make you feel present in the world at first glance.

我希望画面不只是好看，而是能让你一眼置身于这个世界。

Writing文案

I want the story to begin with your choices. After each choice, how will the ending change?

我希望故事从你的选择开始。一次次选择之后，结局会发生怎样的改变？

Game Development游戏开发

I want your actions to leave traces, allowing the world to respond to your presence.

我希望你的行动会留下痕迹，让世界对你的存在作出回应。
