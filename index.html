About 关于

Worlds that begin with your choices. 故事从你的选择开始。

HerRealm Studio creates otome story worlds shaped by character relationships and player choices. The story begins with your choices, and changes because you are there.

她域工作室专注于乙女向游戏，创作由角色关系、玩家选择共同推动的故事世界。 故事从你的选择开始，也因你的存在改变。

The studio is currently in its earliest stage, developing its first original project across writing, visual direction, and interactive systems.

工作室目前处于早期阶段，正在从文案、视觉方向与互动系统三个方面开发第一款原创项目。
